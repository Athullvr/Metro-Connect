How do Metro fares work?+

The Blue Line uses distance zones: ₹10 up to 2 km, then ₹20, ₹30, ₹40, ₹50 and ₹60 above 20 km. This planner estimates a zone from the real station order.

Is the Water Metro tourist-friendly?+

Yes. Fort Kochi is on the operating High Court ↔ Fort Kochi route. Services typically run at 10–20 minute headways; allow a little extra time for the next sailing.

What time does the Metro run?+

Monday–Saturday, 6:00 AM–10:30 PM. On Sundays it starts at 7:30 AM. Check the current operator notice before a late journey.

Is it safe and simple to switch modes?+

Vyttila Mobility Hub is the straightforward integrated interchange. For Fort Kochi, the planner explains the short Ernakulam South → jetty connection in plain language.
